The person behind the work

Angelo — Full-Stack Developer & Founder

5+ years building digital products for startups. Based in Lagos, serving clients across 4 continents.

How it started

A cold email, a broken website, and a lesson that stuck.

In 2019, Angelo cold-reached a local business whose website hadn't loaded correctly in months. The owner didn't know. Angelo fixed it over a weekend — and got paid for the first time in his life from writing code.

That first client became the blueprint. Not design-first, not tech-first — outcome-first. Diagnose what's actually broken. Fix it. Charge for the result, not the effort. The Web Maven was built on that same philosophy: every digital product should earn its keep.

Since then, Angelo has shipped SaaS platforms in the UK, built MVPs that landed paying users before launch day, and integrated AI into products that run on logic, not guesswork. The tools got better. The philosophy didn't change.

How Angelo Works

Three Principles, No Exceptions

"Outcomes over aesthetics."

A beautiful product that doesn't convert is a liability. Every design and engineering decision is evaluated against one question: does this move the business forward? Decoration follows function, not the other way around.

"Ship fast. Iterate faster."

The best product isn't the one with the most features at launch — it's the one in front of real users soonest. Angelo scopes aggressively, ships clean, and iterates based on actual feedback rather than assumptions.

"Business logic first, then code."

The hardest bugs aren't in the code — they're in the requirements. Angelo starts every engagement with a clear model of how the business works before writing a single line. No wasted sprints, no features that don't fit.
