Origin

The cold call that changed everything.

Angelo grew up surrounded by computers. That early immersion gave him a deep, intuitive understanding of digital and analogue systems before most people knew what they were dealing with.

In 2019, he came across a website for a business — it was shabby, non-interactive, not driving traffic, barely functional. Just existing. He reached out to the owners cold and offered to build them something better. He did. That moment changed everything.

Fast-forward: Angelo is now a computer scientist with a body of work spanning AI & machine learning, web development, data science, and automation. He and his team operate under The Web Maven brand — and the emphasis is always on web. Not just design. Not just code. A full digital presence that actually performs.

“Every business deserves a powerful, paying digital presence.”

Based in Lagos, Nigeria — but the reach is global. Current and past clients in Canada, the UK, and the United States. Their systems and processes give them the edge to work seamlessly with clients anywhere on the planet.

The philosophy

Not just design. Not just code. Results.

The Web Maven's approach starts with one question: what should this site actually do for the business? Every layout, every headline, every CTA is in service of that answer — not in service of looking impressive in a portfolio.

01

Strategy First

No designer picks a font before understanding who the customer is and what they need to hear. We don't either.

02

Built to Perform

Speed, accessibility, mobile-first, SEO-ready. A site that looks great but loads slowly or breaks on phones is a liability, not an asset.

03

Outcomes, Always

The measure of a successful project isn't how it looks at launch — it's what it does for the business 6 months later.
