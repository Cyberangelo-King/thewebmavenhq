2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Project type and approximate budget (optional)
  • Your message and project description

This data is submitted to Formspree (our form processing service) and forwarded to our email address.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and page requests as part of their infrastructure. We do not run our own analytics, tracking scripts, or cookies beyond those described in Section 7.

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

  • Payment or financial information
  • Sensitive personal data (health, ethnicity, religion, etc.)
  • Location data beyond your country (inferred from IP by GitHub)
  • Social media login data

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you submit through our contact form solely to:

  • Respond to your project inquiry
  • Discuss scope, pricing, and timelines
  • Communicate with you about your project if we begin working together

We do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise distribute your personal data to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR / NDPR)

For users in the EU/EEA or Nigeria, our legal bases for processing your data are:

  • Legitimate interest — responding to a direct project inquiry you initiated
  • Contractual necessity — where you have engaged us for services
  • Consent — you provide data voluntarily by submitting our form

5. Data Retention

We retain inquiry data for up to 24 months after last contact, unless:

  • You request deletion (see Your Rights below)
  • A contractual relationship exists, in which case we retain records for 6 years per applicable law

Formspree retains form submissions according to their own privacy policy (formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy).

7. Cookies & Local Storage

This website uses no advertising or tracking cookies. We use a single item of localStorage to remember your dark/light mode preference:

  • Key: theme
  • Value: "dark" or "light"
  • Purpose: User experience — persists your display preference across visits
  • Duration: Persists until you clear browser storage or toggle the preference

No personal data is stored in this value. You may clear it at any time via your browser's storage settings. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR (EU), NDPR (Nigeria), and applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — limit how we process your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, email faithakinboyejoa@gmail.com with the subject "Data Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days.

If you are in the EU/EEA, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. Nigerian residents may contact the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) at ndpc.gov.ng.

9. Security

This site is served over HTTPS. We do not store payment data or sensitive personal information. Form submissions are transmitted securely via Formspree's encrypted infrastructure. If you discover a security vulnerability on this site, please report it to faithakinboyejoa@gmail.com.

10. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy
