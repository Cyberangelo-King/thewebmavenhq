Common questions

Before You Reach Out

How long does a project take?

Depends on scope. MVPs typically take 3–6 weeks. Full SaaS platforms run 8–16 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline in the first conversation.

How do payments work?

50% upfront to kick off the project, 50% on final delivery. For longer projects, we can discuss milestone-based payments.

Do you work with non-technical founders?

Yes — most of our clients are. We handle the technical decisions and explain what matters to you in plain language.

What if I need changes after launch?

Two rounds of revisions are included in every project. After that, changes are scoped and quoted separately.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. Monthly retainer options are available for ongoing development, maintenance, and feature additions.

Where are you based? Do you work with international clients?

Based in Lagos, Nigeria. We work with clients in the UK, Canada, USA, and Nigeria. Timezone overlap is rarely an issue.
