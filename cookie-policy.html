Cookie Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026  |  Effective: 3 July 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how The Web Maven ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on this website. We believe in transparency — so this page is short, plain-language, and complete.

Do We Use Cookies?

We do not set any HTTP cookies on your device. This website does not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party cookies of any kind.

What We Do Use: localStorage

We use a single localStorage entry in your browser to remember your display preference (dark or light mode). This is not a cookie — it is stored locally in your browser and is never transmitted to any server.

Name Type Purpose Expires Third Party?
theme localStorage Stores your dark/light mode preference ("dark" or "light") Persists until cleared No

Third-Party Services That May Set Cookies

Google Fonts

This website loads fonts (Inter and Playfair Display) from Google Fonts. When your browser requests a font file, Google's servers may log the request including your IP address. Google may set cookies or use local storage in connection with this request. We do not control Google's cookie behaviour.

Reference: Google Fonts privacy FAQ

Vercel (Hosting)

This site is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may set cookies or log request data as part of their infrastructure. We do not control Vercel's cookie behaviour.

Reference: Vercel Privacy Policy

Formspree (Contact Form)

When you submit our contact form, your data is processed by Formspree. Formspree may set cookies to prevent spam (CSRF protection). We do not control Formspree's cookie behaviour.

Reference: Formspree Privacy Policy

How to Control or Clear Storage

To clear the theme localStorage value:

  • Chrome/Edge: DevTools → Application → Local Storage → delete the theme key
  • Firefox: DevTools → Storage → Local Storage → delete the theme key
  • Safari: Develop → Storage Inspector → Local Storage
  • Or simply: clear your browser's site data for this domain in your browser settings

To block or manage cookies from Google or Vercel, you can use your browser's privacy settings or a browser extension such as uBlock Origin.

Your Consent

Because we do not set cookies, no consent banner is displayed. The theme localStorage value is set only after you click the theme toggle button — it is not set on page load without interaction.

If you would like to object to any storage, you can clear it at any time using the steps above.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever introduce cookies in the future, we will update this policy, add a consent mechanism where required, and update the "Last updated" date above.
